Power-Building

Power-Building is a very distinctive way of training. Unlike Body-Building and Power-Lifting, Power-Building specializes in primarily strength based training while have other accessories after that build sheer mass and size at the same time.

Body-Building

Body-Building is a very well known sport. This type of lifting, is generally only to produce size goals. If you are looking to get bigger and grow a lot of mass than Body-Building is the way to go. It's more of lighter weight training and more isolation movements to grow the muscle rather than strength. Yes, you will get stronger. However, it will be minor.

Power-Lifting

Power-Lifting is a strength sport that consists of three attempts at maximal weight on three lifts. The Squat, Bench press, and Deadlift. As in the sport of Olympic weightlifting, it involves the athlete attempting a maximal weight single-lift effort of a barbell loaded with weight plates.


Experience:

If that is what you are thinking than you are completely wrong. After my years of training I've come to realize that everyone's body is different, some people's may be similarly put together. But everyone is still different. These three programs are to best support you as it supported me. Once i began Power-Building 3 years ago, i have seen nothing but strength and size gains which is the best of both worlds in my book. It is something that I deeply strive for. And you can too!
