About Me

Hello, my name is Devendra Pitam. My goal is to help as much people achieve their fitness goals. Whether it is strength, physique, both or just to live a healthy life-style. I am here to provide different weight-lifting programs for people who are looking to better themselves physically. On top of the programs, there will also be a cookbook and or Diet Plan(s) that can help aid you in your journey and help get results faster. As I always say, you will not achieve goals unless you are committed, everyone has the ability to do something, it's just your mind stopping you. Other than that, diet is crucial. In other words, "eat clean look clean, eat dirty and look dirty." My goal for you all, is to help you gain knowledge about fitness, and better yourselves, so that maybe in the future you can spread the same knowledge to others. And to be comfortable and confident in yourself.
