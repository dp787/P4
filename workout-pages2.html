Bodybuilding Program

During this program, it is more focused on hypertrophy and making sure the mind muscle connection is strong. All the accessories plus main lifts will be listed below:

  • Day 1: Squat Leg Extensions – 5/3/1; Hack Squat – 5×10; Barbell Curl – 5×10; Lateral Raise – 5×10; Incline Curl – 5×10; Cable Rope Pushdown – 5×10
  • Day 2: Bench Press Bench Press – 5/3/1; Bench Press – 5×10; Cable Row – 5×10; Incline Bench Press – 5×10; Wide Grip Lat Pulldown – 5×10; Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 5×10
  • Day 3: Deadlifts Leg Curls – 5/3/1; Romanian Deadlift – 5×10; Close Grip Bench Press – 5×10; Hammer Curl – 5×10; Overhead Tricep Extension – 5×10; Face Pulls – 5×10
  • Day 4: Overhead Press Overhead Press – 5/3/1; Overhead Press – 5×10; Close Grip Lat Pulldown – 5×10; Dumbbell Bench Press – 5×10; Straight Arm Lat Pull Down – 5×10; Cable Flyes – 5×10
  • Day 5: Rest

Note: As always make sure you increase weight weekly. If you feel you can't increase weight the week after, don't feel discouraged, try and do the same weight but do an extra rep or an extra set.

Rest in between sets of course for any exercise. For this program, the goal is to limit the rest period to a minimum. Meaning take less time breaks in between sets. So instead of a 2 minute break, do a 45 second break.
